# Privacy Policy - JobF1 for LinkedIn

JobF1 for LinkedIn is a Chrome extension that helps users filter LinkedIn job listings by posting time, keywords, job type, and visa sponsorship indicators.

## Information We Collect

JobF1 for LinkedIn does not collect personal data for storage on any external server. The extension works locally in the browser.

- The extension stores user-selected filter preferences such as time filter, job type, keywords, and sponsorship settings.
- These settings may be saved using Chrome storage so they can persist across browser sessions.
- When you view LinkedIn job pages, the extension reads job title, company name, and job description text on the page to determine whether sponsorship-related restrictions may be present.

## What We Do Not Do

- We do not collect names, email addresses, passwords, or payment details.
- We do not send browsing data or job page content to our own servers.
- We do not sell or transfer user data to third parties.
- We do not use user data for advertising, profiling, or credit decisions.

## Permissions

The extension requests browser permissions only to apply filters on LinkedIn job pages, save settings, and analyze the content of LinkedIn job listings locally in the browser.

## Data Retention

Saved preferences remain in Chrome storage until the user removes the extension or clears browser data. We do not maintain a separate backend database.
